IBKR ForecastTrader lets EEA MiFID retail clients1 and MiFID Professional clients2 use USD-denominated exchange-listed Forecast Contracts to trade their opinion on yes-or-no questions on economic, financial and climate indicators. By investing in a Forecast Contract, you can express your conviction on or hedge against the outcome of key economic and climate events. Forecast Contracts provide a direct method for protecting a portfolio from the volatility associated with these events.

How do Forecast Contracts work?

If you believe that an event will occur, such as an increase in the US Consumer Price Index above a specific value or a change in global temperatures, you can buy a "Yes" contract. If you think the event will not occur, you can purchase a "No" contract.

The value of these contracts will fluctuate based on market sentiment and evolving probabilities. Upon the contract's resolution, the contract settles at a predefined value:

  • USD 1 for a correct prediction
  • USD 0 for an incorrect prediction

Contract holders will also be paid an incentive coupon based upon the daily closing price of each contract, currently at USD 3.83% APY.*

Forecast Contracts are available on a variety of events such as:

  • US Fed Funds Rate
  • US Consumer Sentiment
  • US Housing Starts
  • US Retail Sales
  • US Building Permits
  • US Consumer Price Index
  • US Payroll Employment
  • Global Temperatures
  • US Temperatures

Forecast Contracts trade around the clock from 3:00 am ET Sunday to 10:30 pm ET Friday with a 15 minute break at 5:00 pm ET, and can be traded on any of our desktop and mobile platforms or via IBKR ForecastTrader, our easy-to-use web platform that can be accessed with your existing login credentials.

If you do not have Forecast Contracts trading permission but would like to trade your opinion, please log in to Portal to request Forecast Contracts trading permission.

  1. Log in to Portal
  2. Select the User ("head/shoulders" icon) > Settings
  3. Under Trading, select Trading Permissions
  4. Select Forecast and Event Contracts - Request
  5. Select Request Permissions
  6. Read and acknowledge legal documents and disclosures
  7. Select OK

Trading permission upgrade requests are subject to Compliance review and are typically approved in 1 - 2 days.
