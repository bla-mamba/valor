CME Group Micro Bitcoin Futures Contracts Available at IBKR

Interactive Brokers now offers access to CME Group Micro Bitcoin futures contracts. At 1/10 the size of one bitcoin, Micro Bitcoin futures (MBT) provide an efficient, cost-effective way to fine-tune bitcoin exposure and enhance your trading strategies. Enjoy the features of Bitcoin futures (BTC) in a smaller slice that gives active traders more choices for managing bitcoin price risk.

  • TRADE A SLICE OF BITCOIN FUTURES:
    Get the same features as the larger five-bitcoin contract, at 1/50 of its contract size, settled to the regulated CME CF Bitcoin Reference Rate (MBT).
  • PRICE DISCOVERY AND TRANSPARENCY:
    Trade on a regulated exchange and enjoy the price discovery of transparent futures, where all participants see the same prices and quotes.
  • PRECISELY SCALE BITCOIN EXPOSURE:
    Add more control over the amount of bitcoin exposure in your trading strategies.
  • CAPITAL EFFICIENCY IN CRYPTO TRADING:
    Save on potential margin offsets with Bitcoin futures and options and Ether futures, plus add the efficiency of futures contracts.

If you already have Futures trading permissions, you can trade CME Group Micro Bitcoin Futures.

To trade from Trader Workstation (TWS), create an order ticket, type "MBT" in the Financial Instrument search field, enter your order details, and click "Transmit."

From Portal, click Trade > Futures, search for "MBT", enter all order details, and click "Submit Buy Order" to place the trade.

If you would like to request Futures trading permissions, log in to the Settings > Account Settings Trading Experience & Permissions page in Client Portal.

Earn Extra Income on Fully Paid Shares in the Stock Yield Enhancement Program

Interactive Brokers' ("IB LLC") Stock Yield Enhancement Program allows you to earn extra income on fully paid shares of stock in your account*. Under the program, IB LLC borrows shares of stock from you and provides you with collateral until those shares are returned. While the shares are on loan, you earn a percentage of the loan income generated from IB LLC on-lending your shares.

Interactive Brokers manages all aspects of share lending. While your shares are on loan you still own the stock, which means you continue to have market risk and will recognize any profit (or loss) if the stock price moves. You can sell your shares at any time without restriction and can terminate your participation at any time for any reason.

To participate in the Program:

  • Log in to Client Portal
  • Navigate to Settings > Account Settings (Home page footer)
  • Click the "Configure" icon for "Stock Yield Enhancement Program"
  • Click the check box to enroll in the program
  • Click Continue
  • Review disclosures and sign agreement
  • Click Continue

Interactive Advisors Offers Thirteen Socially Responsible Investing Portfolios

Interactive Advisors offers the following thirteen Socially Responsible Investing Portfolios, each seeking to invest in a basket of stocks with favorable Socially Responsible Investing characteristics and criteria by relying on systematic rules-based investment strategies that do not use conventional market capitalization weights.

  • Clean Air Portfolio: Designed to invest in environmentally conscious companies committed to reducing emissions, managing the sustainability of their energy consumption, and having a low-carbon footprint.
  • Company Transparency Portfolio: Designed to invest in companies that make an effort to protect their customers, by focusing on customer welfare and data security, and following best practices regarding product safety, quality and product labeling.
  • Customer Safety Portfolio: Designed to invest in companies that care about customer privacy and safety, product access and affordability, product quality and safety, and follow good selling practices.
  • Ethical Leadership Portfolio: Designed to invest in companies that follow best-practice corporate governance, competitive behavior, and risk management principles, and provide equal treatment of stakeholders.
  • Fair Labor & Thriving Communities Portfolio: Designed to invest in companies that care about the societies where they operate as well as maintain diverse and equal opportunities, and development opportunities for their workforce.
  • Gender Equality Portfolio: Designed to invest in companies that maintain diverse and equal opportunities, and development opportunities for their workforce.
  • LGBTQ Inclusion Portfolio: Designed to invest in companies that maintain diverse and equal opportunities, and development opportunities for their LGBTQ workforce.
  • Land Health Portfolio: Designed to invest in companies that pay attention to harmful land waste as a byproduct of their activities and take into consideration the safe treatment, storage, and disposal of solid waste while observing local and universal regulations.
  • Mindful Business Models Portfolio: Designed to invest in companies that display business model resilience and incorporate ESG elements into long-term planning.
  • Ocean Life Portfolio: Designed to invest in companies that manage their water use and place value on ocean and marine life.
  • Pure Water Portfolio: Designed to invest in companies that manage their water use and avoid contamination and toxic waste.
  • Racial Equality Portfolio: Designed to invest in companies that maintain diverse and equal opportunities, and development opportunities for their workforce.
  • Sustainable Product Lifecycle Portfolio: Designed to invest in companies that consider the design, quality, and safety of products and services, focusing on the entire supply chain and the materials used in manufacturing products.
